TL;DR

Write Javascript code to manage the emergency room.

Rules

30 of them are infectious. You can call these patients into any rooms. But be careful! An infectious patient will infect all other patient inside a room. Each new infection.

Rules

You are the Staff Executive Parking Manager at VÃ¡ci Greens. Every morning 100 cars arrive, you have to redirect them into one of the 3 parking lots with 30-30-30 parking spaces.

20 of them are managers. Every manager who gets into the upper floor of the underground garage means 10 points for you. You do not receive points for parking on an other floor or overground.

30 of them are disabled. They prefer parking spaces near the entrance in the overground parking lots. You can receive 1 to 10 points, 10 points for the first, 1 point for the 10th parking place, in the order of arrivals. If they have to park farther or underground, you do not receive points.

The rest, employees. are simple: you gain 2 points for each of them who can park in any place.

  • Write plain Javascript (ES6) code, do not use any framework or library.
  • Your code MUST always choose a parking lot for each car. If you send a car into a parking lot which is full, that car won't park, it will go away.
  • Your code MUST never throw an error or exception.

How to play

Clone https://github.com/elcsiga/emergency-room-challenge . Open ERManager.js and write code. Test your code by opening index.html in your browser.
